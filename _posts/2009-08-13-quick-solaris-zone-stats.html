--- published: true status: publish layout: post meta: _edit_last: "2" type: post tags: - OpenSolaris - Unix - Unix / Linux title: Quick Solaris zone stats category: UNIX / Linux --- Add this: 
alias zonestat="prstat -vZ 1 1 | grep -A50 '^ZONEID'"
to your ~/.profile and you should see something like this when running it: 
root@tank:~# zonestat
